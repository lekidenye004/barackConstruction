Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a quote?
We typically provide initial quotes within 24-48 hours after consultation. Complex projects may take 3-5 business days for detailed quotations.
Do you offer free consultations?
Yes! We offer free initial consultations to understand your project requirements and provide preliminary advice and budget estimates.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all major areas in Kenya including Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and surrounding regions. We also handle projects in other counties upon request.
Do you handle both small and large projects?
Absolutely! We handle projects of all sizes, from small renovations and home extensions to large commercial complexes and infrastructure projects.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. Flexible payment plans are available for large projects.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes! We are fully licensed by the National Construction Authority (NCA) and fully insured for all our projects.
