About Premium Constructions

Building Kenya's future with excellence, integrity, and innovation since 2010

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Kenya's most trusted construction companies

Premium Constructions was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate engineers and architects with a shared vision: to revolutionize the construction industry in Kenya through quality, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

What started as a small family business with just 5 dedicated professionals has grown into a leading construction company with over 50 expert team members, having successfully completed 500+ projects across residential, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, learning, and a steadfast commitment to delivering exceptional results. Every project we undertake is a testament to our dedication to quality craftsmanship, sustainable practices, and client satisfaction.
